About Post-Concussion Care

What is Post-Concussion & Headache Care After Accidents?

Many accident victims experience headaches, dizziness, and cognitive fog after a crash — even without a direct head impact. Cervicogenic headaches (originating from the neck) are often misdiagnosed as concussion and respond exceptionally well to chiropractic care.

The cervical spine plays a critical role in headaches and dizziness. Misalignment and muscle tension in the neck after a crash can mimic or worsen concussion symptoms.

Without proper chiropractic treatment, whiplash frequently becomes a chronic condition that affects daily life for months or years.

⚡ Critical Timing:

Whiplash treated within 72 hours of the accident has a significantly better recovery outcome than whiplash treated weeks later. And it's 100% free — there is literally no reason to wait.

Our Treatment Approach
How We Treat Post-Concussion Care — Evidence-Based Protocol
Our post-concussion care treatment protocol is designed specifically for motor vehicle accident injuries — not adapted from general chiropractic care.
1

Comprehensive Assessment

Full cervical spine evaluation, range of motion testing, orthopedic tests, and neurological screening. We identify all injured structures — not just where it hurts.

2

Spinal Manipulation

Precise, gentle adjustments to the cervical and thoracic spine to restore proper alignment, reduce nerve irritation, and improve mobility.

3

Soft Tissue Therapy

Myofascial release, trigger point therapy, and instrument-assisted techniques to break down scar tissue and reduce muscle spasm in the neck and upper back.

4

Therapeutic Exercise

Progressive rehabilitation exercises to strengthen the deep neck stabilizers and restore normal movement patterns — preventing chronic whiplash syndrome.

5

Progress Documentation

Detailed visit records tracking your recovery, supporting your insurance claim and any personal injury legal proceedings.
