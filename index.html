Don't Wait — Accident Injuries Get Worse Without Treatment

Adrenaline after a crash masks pain. Many patients feel fine for 24–72 hours — then wake up unable to move. The longer you wait, the harder injuries are to treat and document.

  • Untreated injuries lead to chronic pain and long-term damage
