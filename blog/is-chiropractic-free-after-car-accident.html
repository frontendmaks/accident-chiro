Is Chiropractic Care Really Free After a Car Accident in Oregon?

Yes — for most Oregon accident victims, chiropractic care after a car crash costs absolutely nothing. Here's exactly how Oregon's insurance laws work and what you're entitled to.

One of the biggest misconceptions about auto accident injuries is that chiropractic treatment is expensive or requires out-of-pocket payment. In Oregon, this simply isn't true for most accident victims. Here's why.

Oregon's PIP Law: The Basics

Oregon requires every auto insurance policy to include Personal Injury Protection (PIP) coverage. This is mandatory — you have it whether you know it or not.

PIP coverage pays for medical expenses — including chiropractic care — for you and your passengers after an accident, regardless of who was at fault. You don't need to prove the other driver caused the accident to use your PIP benefits.

✅ Oregon Minimum PIP Coverage

Oregon law requires a minimum of $15,000 in PIP medical coverage per person. Many policies carry $25,000–$100,000. For chiropractic care, $15,000 covers months of treatment for most patients.

How Much Does Chiropractic Cost After an Accident?

With Oregon PIP: $0 for most patients.

We bill your insurance company directly. You provide your insurance information, we handle all the paperwork and billing. You focus entirely on recovery.

What if the Accident Was My Fault?

PIP covers you regardless of fault. If you rear-ended someone, your own PIP still pays for your chiropractic treatment. This is a critical misunderstanding — many accident victims don't seek treatment because they think their insurance won't cover them if they caused the accident.

What if I Don't Have PIP or My Policy Lapsed?

In this case, the at-fault driver's liability insurance may cover your treatment. Or you may be able to proceed with treatment under a letter of protection with a personal injury attorney — meaning treatment costs are deferred until your case settles.

What About Deductibles?

Oregon PIP has a maximum deductible of $250. Many policies have a $0 deductible for PIP medical benefits. Even with a $250 deductible, your total treatment cost for months of chiropractic care may be just $250.

⚠️ File Promptly — PIP Has Time Limits

You must notify your insurance company within a reasonable time after the accident to preserve your PIP rights. "Reasonable" under Oregon law is generally interpreted as within 14 days. Don't wait.
