SW Murray Blvd, SW Millikan Way, and the surrounding streets near Nike World HQ and Adidas North America generate extreme shift-change congestion. Cedar Hills Blvd & TV Hwy — Commercial Corridor

The Cedar Hills Crossing mall area creates classic commercial district accident conditions — high pedestrian volume, parking lot pull-outs, U-turns, and distracted drivers. This intersection sees some of Beaverton's highest T-bone and angle crash rates, particularly on weekends and evenings.

SW Canyon Rd — Two-City Connector

Canyon Road connecting Beaverton to Portland's West Hills carries a mix of residential and commuter traffic through winding, lower-visibility stretches. Speed differential crashes — where faster-moving vehicles collide with slowing traffic around blind curves — are especially common near the Beaverton-Hillsdale Hwy junction.
